CBCL是由麻省理工学院生物与计算学习中心公开的供科研与学习的数据库，它包含人脸数据集和非人脸数据集两部分，并且分别分为训练集和测试集两部分。其中，人脸训练集有2429幅19*19尺度的灰度图片，测试集有472幅；而非人脸训练集包含4548幅19*19尺度的灰度图片，及测试集有23573张。在该文中，人脸数据集中每张人脸图片可以表示成一幅19*19的行列矩阵，然后将其按列拉直成一个361*1的向量；故此，人脸数据集可以编码成361*2429+361*472（训练集+测试集）尺度的矩阵，每一列代表一幅灰度人脸图片。具体的数据参看CBCL.mat文件（train=（361*2429），test=（361*361），pic_rows=19，pic_cols=19）。

CBCL is a public database for research usage provided by the MIT center for Biological and Computation Learning. This dataset contains face archives and non-face corpus, which was divided into training and testing parts respectively. More specifically, the face image-set holds 2429 and 472 grayscale pictures corresponding to the training and testing sets with 19*19 dimensions for each; while there are 4548 and 23573 images accordingly in the non-face section. In this context, each face could be converted to a 19*19 matrix, and further be vectorized into a 361*1 vector; Consequently, the whole face corpus equates to two separate matrices: 361*2429 (training set) and 361*472(testing set), and each column represents one face image. The concrete digital datasets are coded in CBCL.mat with variables train (361*2429), test (361*472), pic_rows (19) and pic_cols (19).
