Sogou-C5-500摘取自搜狗实验室整理发布的搜狐新闻语料。搜狐新闻语料经过编辑手工进行整理与分类，包括国内，国际，体育，社会，娱乐等18类新闻主题的内容。其中Sogou-C5-500摘取了其中的互联网、健康、军事、旅游、财经共5类新闻主题各500篇新闻内容，并经过非中文字符过滤、使用Ansj中文分词进行分词、过滤停止词、抽取名词和动词作为词干、筛选词频等操作进行处理。一条新闻内容可以表示成一行以空格分隔的词语的组合，从而形成500*5行的内容，详见Sogou-C5-500.txt文件。对应的词典可见wordMap.txt，统计信息见sizeInfo.txt，每行内容的类标见label.txt。此外，Sogou-C5-500数据集还计算出各个词语的tfidf信息，并生成出词语数量*文档数（14921*2500）的行列矩阵，见tfidf.txt文件。稀疏表示的tfidf信息见tfidf_sparse.txt文件。

Sogou-C5-500 is a public corpus for research usage. This corpus was excerpted from the Sohu news provided by Sogou Lab. After being edited and classificated manually, there are 18 topics in the Sohu news including Chinese news, international news, sports news, social news, entertainment news, etc. Sogou-C5-500 contains 500 news each topic in 5 topics with internet news, health news, military news, travel news and financial news. There are 5*500 lines in the corpus where each line represents the content of one news after removing the non-Chinese character, segmenting Chinese words by Ansj tool, getting rid of the stop words, keeping the nouns and verbs, filtering by words' frequency. The concrete digital corpus is coded in Sogou-C5-500.txt, the dictionary information about the corpus is in wordMap.txt, the size information is in sizeInfo.txt, the label information is in label.txt. Sogou-C5-500 also provides the tfidf information in tfidf.txt with 1492*2500 dimensions and the sparse representation of tfidf matrix is in tfidf_sparse.txt.

For more information about Sohu news, you can refer to http://www.sogou.com/labs/resource/cs.php
